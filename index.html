Connecting minds, not only layers.

About Us

We are the first Machine Learning student association at Bocconi University and our goal is to connect people sharing the same passion and goals.

BSML is a launching pad where members can showcase their projects, nurture ideas, and find collaborators to pursue their ambitions.

BSML is not built by its members, BSML is its members.

Research Projects

Exploring modern topics of machine learning and artificial intelligence through hands-on projects.

Coding Challenges

Hosting machine learning challenges for members with real data.

Articles

Writing articles on the latest news and summaries of basic machine learning topics

Podcast

Interesting talks with students, professors and experts from the field.

Open Roles

We have some projects going and we need your help.

We list here some profile we would love to welcome in the association, please apply if you are interested!

  • Web Developer

  • Content Creator

  • Project Lead

Description

We are migrating our website from a third-party provider to a in-house and self-built solution hosted through GitHub Pages. Do you want to contribute to the actual code writing? Do you have suggestions to improve the website?

Day-to-Day

You will be working closely with our senior members to create website's sections from scratch: your laptop, your hands, your code.

Recipe for Success

  • Knowledge about HTML-CSS-JS and Bootstrap5
  • Proactivity and attitude towards collaborations
  • Propose ideas and implement prototypes
